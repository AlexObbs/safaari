Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

Kenya On A Budget Safaris uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Necessary Cookies: These enable basic functions and are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Functional Cookies: These help us remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website, which allows us to improve it.
  • Marketing Cookies: These are used to track visitors across websites to enable us to display relevant advertisements.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on "Cookie Settings" at the bottom of our website. You can also delete cookies that have already been set in your browser settings.

Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics (for website usage analysis)
  • Firebase (for authentication and database services)
  • Stripe (for payment processing)

How Long Will Cookies Remain

The length of time a cookie remains on your device depends on whether it is a "persistent" or "session" cookie:

  • Session cookies last only until you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies remain until they expire or are deleted.
