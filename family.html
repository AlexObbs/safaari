The Ultimate 9-Day Kenya Family Adventure

Discover Kenya’s breathtaking landscapes, iconic wildlife, and vibrant culture with our expertly curated family itinerary. From thrilling safaris to relaxing coastal escapes, this all-inclusive adventure is designed to leave every family member with unforgettable memories—all at an unbeatable price.

Day 1

Arrival and Welcome to Kenya.

Experience Begins: Upon arrival at the airport, a dedicated guide will welcome you with warm Kenyan hospitality.
Personal Touch: A private transfer will take your family to a cozy BnB where a beautifully arranged food bouquet awaits to make you feel right at home.
Relax & Unwind: Take the evening to rest, recharge, and prepare for the adventure ahead.

Day 2

Wildlife Wonders & Family Fun

Morning Magic: Kickstart your day at the Giraffe Centre, where you’ll hand-feed and interact with the majestic creatures in a serene environment.
Lunch with a View: Enjoy a delightful meal near the Nairobi National Park, surrounded by the sounds of nature.
Educational Adventure: Head to the Sheldrick Wildlife Trust to meet baby elephants, learn about their rescue stories, and witness conservation in action.
Fun for All: End the day at Two Rivers Mall, where the kids can enjoy carnival rides and activities. Meanwhile, parents can sneak away to a luxurious spa retreat, knowing the little ones are in good hands with a vetted nanny.

Day 3

Thrills at Hells Gate and Naivasha Serenity

Bike Through Nature: Cycle through the breathtaking landscapes of Hells Gate National Park, with stunning rock formations, wildlife sightings, and a guide van ready to assist if needed.
Rejuvenate in Warm Waters: After the thrill of biking, soak in the natural Olkaria Geothermal Spa, a one-of-a-kind experience.
Sundowner on the Lake: Cap off the day with a relaxing boat ride on Lake Naivasha, where you’ll spot playful hippos and enjoy the magical Kenyan sunset.

Day 4

Immerse in Kenyan Culture

Authentic Connections: Spend the day with local artisans and chefs, learning how to prepare Kenya’s most loved cuisines. Try your hand at crafting souvenirs with the guidance of talented locals.
Evening Bonfire: Gather around a traditional bonfire and enjoy an unforgettable Nyama Choma feast while swapping stories and soaking in Kenyan culture under the stars.

Day 5

The Ultimate Maasai Mara Safari

Journey to the Wild: Take a scenic drive or train ride to the world-famous Maasai Mara, home to the Big Five and stunning savannah landscapes.
Afternoon Game Drive: Experience the thrill of spotting lions, elephants, and cheetahs on an expert-guided safari tour.
Under the Stars: Spend the night in a luxury tented camp, surrounded by the sounds of the African wilderness.

Day 6

Morning Safari & Coastal Charm

Sunrise Safari: Start the day with a magical morning game drive as the savannah comes to life.
Coastal Bound: Enjoy breakfast before heading to Mombasa via flight or train. Dine by the Ocean: End the day with a relaxing dinner overlooking the Indian Ocean, indulging in fresh seafood and coastal delicacies.

Day 7

Coastal Adventure

Marine Exploration: Visit the beautiful Malindi or take an exciting trip to the Dolphin Marine Park to witness playful dolphins in their natural habitat.
Coastal Cuisine: Savor exotic coastal dishes, blending African, Arabic, and Indian influences.

Day 8

Rest and Return to Nairobi

Relax and Reflect: Spend the morning unwinding on the beach or exploring Mombasa’s vibrant streets.
Journey Back: Travel back to Nairobi via road, train, or flight.
Farewell Dinner: Wrap up your trip with a farewell dinner at a charming Nairobi restaurant.

Day 9

Farewell, Kenya!

Goodbye, but not Forever: Transfer to the airport for your departure flight, taking home memories of Kenya’s incredible landscapes, wildlife, and warm hospitality.
